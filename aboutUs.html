ABOUT US

WHO ARE WE


Shotokan Karate Fedaration India is working working towards the mainstream integration of marginalized sections of society, especially women and girl children, through self-sustaining projects that focus on psychological, social and professional training and rehabilitation. Over the years, our main goal was and continues to be to empower women and girls from poorer and under-privileged sections of society by equipping them with skills and confidence, that allows them to live their lives with dignity and security.




OUR VISION

To envision and eventually live in a world without violence against women and children.




OUR MISSION

To educate individuals, families, communities and corporations on violence prevention and protection against abuse.
